Nnedi Okorafor's Novels

Book Jacket Design, 2019

Using a contemporary author’s books titles, I was tasked to design a minimum of three different titled hard-cover book jackets that establishes a line look for the series.

The author I chose was Nnedi Okorafor, an award-winning Nigerian-American writer of science fiction and fantasy stories for children and adults. Her three book titles that I redesigned were Akata Witch, Who Fears Death, and Binti.

The books have different genres and themes, meaning the designs were unique to each book. I was able to connect the three jacket designs together by using a circle as a way to represent the main characters. Overall though, the look I envisioned for the book jackets were geometric, abstract, and minimal so it can be open to different interpretations.
