Subway Voices

Graphic Interactive Experience, 2019

For this project, I was tasked to express/extend the formal, aesthetic, thematic, or functional aspect of a previous work I've done through coding. By using Processing, I needed to create a sketch to render a 2D image that serves as an impression of my previous work.

The previous work I chose was a document that used typography to visualize the sounds and noises recorded from a specific location.

Using my phone, I recorded the audio while I rode the TTC subway on Line 1 Yonge-University from Saint Patrick Station to Eglinton West Station.

The result was an interactive design that allowed the user drag to the mouse in order to experience being in the subway while “travelling between stations”. Dragging the mouse showcases the columns of automated robotic overhead voices in the background, while words said by people are floating randomly around the “subway”, or the mouse.
