Bacardi ROJO Spiced Rum

Packaging Design, 2020

The task for this project is to design an upscale subbrand and packaging for a new Bacardi Spiced Rum drink. ‘ROJO’, meaning blood in Spanish, represents the blood spilled in Cuba’s history of independence against Spain — and the struggle that the Bacardi family had endured.

The concept of ‘ROJO’ is depicted as the ‘ROJO Blood Crystal’, which shines brightly against the red amber rum. Based on geometric forms, the overall design exudes a modern, edgy, and sophisticated feel, perfect for a younger target audience who wants a warm, intense, and spicy drink.

The Bacardi ‘ROJO’ Spiced Rum gift box is a simpler expression of the bottle. It is three-sided to emulate the forms of the triangles and the ‘blood crystal.’ I designed the box to include a die-cut where the bottle would sit. In doing so, the bottle can be seen through the opening. The top and bottom flaps would be glued together to create a sealed safe space for the bottle.

Ideation

My thought process behind these sketches is to use a figure that was symbolic to Cuba. At first, I wanted to connect ‘ROJO’ with vampiric bats, but the local traditions actually admire bats. So, I've employed the concept of blood itself as the main graphic element to represent the history of Bacardi and Cuba.
