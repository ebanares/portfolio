Atomic Bombing of Hiroshima

Triptich, 2019

for this project, i was tasked to choose a recognizable event in human history and represent it in a triptych. i chose the atomic bombing of hiroshima as it was the first time that humanity showed that it was capable of indiscriminate mass destruction. this significant moment in history set off a reaction that drove the rest of the 20th century.

typography, imagery, and symbolism.

these were essential to describing the events that took place on that day in each of the triptych’s panels.

the main goal was to tell the story of the bombing and evoke the feelings of loss, grief, chaos, and destruction. these emotions are shown through the use of dark colours, graphic imagery, and strong language in the form of real quotes sourced from the victims of the attack.
