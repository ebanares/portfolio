Lady Gaga x Adobe Creative Cloud

Poster Design, 2020

This project was a creative challenge by Adobe Creative Cloud in collaboration with Lady Gaga and her newest album, Chromatica (2020). The challenge was to produce a static poster showing how I experience Chromatica using, at minimum, graphic elements from the album such as typography, photography, and icons.

Ideation and Mockups

One of my all-time favourite artists has to be Lady Gaga and it is an honour to be able to express myself and my love for this new album through Adobe. Chromatica to me is exciting, electrifying, and uplifting. I listen to it when I am feeling down or just want to jump up and dance. I wanted to portray these feelings and combine it with the grungy, dystopian aesthetic that is prevalent in the songs and music videos, such as Stupid Love and Rain On Me.

The sketches are not easily understandable but their concepts were inspired by the Machine City seen in The Matrix Revolutions (2003) and an animation by Anthony F. Schepperd for the song The Music Scene by Blockhead.

These initial mockups were left unfinished as they did not come close to fulfilling my ideas. However, I chose to include them to show my progression from sketches to initial designs to the final mockup.
