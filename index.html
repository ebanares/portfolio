ABOUT

My name is Eldrin Banares. I’m currently a fourth-year graphic design student at OCAD University. I often focus on typography and its application in print or digital media using Adobe Creative Suite programs, such as InDesign, Illustrator, and AfterEffects. Recently, I'm learning more about programming for web design or for graphic and interactive experiences. I'm also interested in drawing and painting once in a while.

If you're interested in connecting or working together, say hello.
