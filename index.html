ABOUT

Hello! I’m Eldrin Banares. I’m currently a fourth-year Graphic Design student at OCAD University.

My goal in life as a designer is to have fun and be expressive with everything I create for myself or with others. I’m especially interested in producing interactive coding experiences, advertising, publications, motion graphics, and kinetic typography.

Feel free to reach out and say hello!
